# /monday-report

Call `get_weekly_report` (Hoxo connector) — this returns the full Monday Standup already generated by the system.

If the report is marked as mocked or empty, tell the user:
> Your standup hasn't been generated yet. The system runs every Monday at 8am. Type /monday-report again after 8am, or contact your Hoxo team if it's missing.

---

## What to do with the report

Present it section by section. After presenting each section, pause and ask which parts they want to act on. Don't dump the whole thing at once unless they ask.

Suggested flow:
1. Present **Intelligence Report** — "Here's what's been happening in your market this week."
2. Present **Content Pipeline** — "Here are your 10 post ideas for this week. Which 2 are you going to write?"
3. Present **Podcast Agent** — "Here are 10 guest prospects with personalised invitation drafts."
4. Present **ICP Outreach Packs** — "Here are 10 outreach packs. Which 5 are you sending today?"
5. Present **Performance Lens** — "Here's what worked last week and why."
6. Present **Daily Orchestrator** — "And here's who engaged with your content yesterday — these are the warmest leads right now."

If they want to act immediately (e.g. "write the first post", "send this outreach DM"), do it using their voice from `voice-samples/` and ICP from `get_icp` (Hoxo connector).
